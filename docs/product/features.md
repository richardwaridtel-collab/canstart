# Core Product Features – CanStart

---

## FOR JOB SEEKERS

### 1. Profile Creation & Management

**What:** Build a complete profile showing work history, skills, languages, availability

**Why It Matters:**
- Employers need to understand who you are before matching/hiring
- Profile data powers the matching algorithm
- Good profile = more matches and job offers

**How It Works:**
- Step-by-step form (education → experience → skills → languages → preferences)
- Upload profile photo
- Add work history with dates and descriptions
- Tag skills and list fluency levels
- Set location preference and availability

**Key Fields:**
- Name, contact, location
- Work history (company, role, dates, achievements)
- Education (school, degree, graduation date)
- Skills (searchable, tagged by proficiency)
- Languages (with fluency levels: beginner/intermediate/fluent)
- Certifications (if any)
- Availability (start date, full-time/part-time preference)
- Location preference (willing to relocate? specific cities?)

### 2. Job Search & Browsing

**What:** Find relevant jobs easily with filters and search

**Why It Matters:**
- Reduce time spent browsing irrelevant jobs
- Find opportunities matching your skills and location
- See all opportunities at a glance

**How It Works:**
- Browse all open jobs (sorted by relevance/recency)
- Filter by: location, job type, industry, skills required
- View job details: description, requirements, salary, location
- See match score (how well job fits your profile)
- Save/bookmark jobs for later

**Key Features:**
- Advanced search by job title, company, industry
- Filter by: location radius, work type (volunteer/paid), salary range
- Sort by: relevance, newest, most applications
- View match percentage for each job
- Save favorites for later review
- Get notified when similar jobs posted

### 3. Job Application

**What:** Apply to jobs directly through platform

**Why It Matters:**
- One-click apply (fast)
- Employer sees your full profile automatically
- All applications tracked in one place
- No need to copy/paste into separate forms

**How It Works:**
- Click "Apply" on job listing
- Platform automatically sends application with your profile to employer
- Can add optional cover letter
- Receive confirmation that application was sent
- Track application status (pending/viewed/interviewing/rejected/offered)

**Key Features:**
- Quick-apply (automatic submission with profile)
- Optional cover letter field
- Application history (see all applications and statuses)
- Withdrawal option (can withdraw application)
- Reminders to follow up if no response in 1 week

### 4. Messaging & Communication

**What:** Message employers directly to discuss role, ask questions, schedule interviews

**Why It Matters:**
- Clear communication speeds up hiring process
- All conversations in one place
- Secure platform (reduces scam risk)
- Employer contact info secured on platform (not exposed directly)

**How It Works:**
- Receive message notification when employer responds
- Reply directly in-app
- Schedule interview times (calendar integration)
- Share documents/attachments if needed
- See full conversation history

**Key Features:**
- In-app direct messaging
- Email notifications for new messages
- Message read receipts
- Calendar integration for interview scheduling
- Conversation history searchable
- Attachment sharing (documents, references)
- Suggested response templates

### 5. Verification

**What:** Verify you are who you say you are and have legal work authorization

**Why It Matters:**
- Gives employers confidence to hire you
- Shows you're a legitimate, serious job seeker
- Increases your chances of getting interviews
- Gets you "Verified" badge on profile

**How It Works:**
- Submit ID (passport, driver's license, or national ID)
- Upload proof of work permit (LMIA, PGWP, OWP, etc.)
- Admin reviews and approves (24-48 hours)
- Get "Verified" badge on your profile

**Key Features:**
- Secure document upload
- Privacy protected (documents not visible to employers)
- Verification status displayed prominently
- Notification when verified
- Re-verify if documents expire

### 6. Ratings & Feedback

**What:** Rate employers after completing a job; help others make informed decisions

**Why It Matters:**
- Builds community trust
- Warns others about bad employers
- Rewards good employers with better candidates
- Accountability for employers

**How It Works:**
- After job completion (or 30 days after hire), receive prompt to rate employer
- 5-star rating on: professionalism, work environment, pay, learning opportunity
- Optional written feedback (what went well, what could improve)
- Can see aggregated ratings from other job seekers before applying
- Ratings appear on employer profile (anonymous, aggregated)

**Key Features:**
- 5-star rating system with defined criteria
- Anonymous feedback (employer sees rating, not identity)
- Written feedback (optional, max 500 words)
- "Verified Hire" badge after completing role
- Helpful/not helpful voting on reviews

## FOR EMPLOYERS

### 1. Company Profile & Verification

**What:** Create employer profile showing company info and verify you're a legitimate business

**Why It Matters:**
- Job seekers need confidence you're real (prevents scams)
- Profile shows candidate what your company is like
- Verified badge builds trust with candidates
- Professional appearance attracts better applicants

**How It Works:**
- Create profile with: company name, size, industry, location, website
- Upload business registration document
- Write company description (mission, culture, why join us)
- Admin verifies business registration (24-48 hours)
- Get "Verified Employer" badge

**Key Features:**
- Company information (size, industry, founding date)
- Logo upload
- Company description (culture, values, mission)
- Website link
- Contact information
- Verification badge display
- Company rating (aggregated from employee feedback)

### 2. Job Posting

**What:** Post open positions and attract qualified candidates

**Why It Matters:**
- Reach pre-qualified candidate pool (newcomers wanting to work)
- Simplified posting vs. LinkedIn/Indeed
- Target exactly the role you need
- Track applicants easily

**How It Works:**
- Fill out job posting form: title, description, requirements, salary, location
- Select work type: full-time, part-time, volunteer
- Choose skills required (system suggests from job title)
- Set salary range (optional, but helps attract better candidates)
- Publish job
- Edit or delete job anytime
- Receive notifications when candidates apply

**Key Features:**
- Easy job posting form (10 minutes to complete)
- Job templates by role (tech, healthcare, admin, retail, etc.)
- Skill suggestions (system recommends required skills)
- Salary guidance (market research data)
- Bulk posting option (CSV upload for multiple jobs)
- Post expiration date (jobs auto-expire after 60 days)
- Repost previous jobs (one-click)

### 3. Candidate Search

**What:** Browse qualified candidates and find matches for your open roles

**Why It Matters:**
- Access large pool of motivated job seekers
- Pre-vetted candidates (credentials verified)
- No need to advertise; platform does matching
- Reduces screening time dramatically

**How It Works:**
- View candidates matching your job posting
- Filter by: skills, experience level, location, availability
- See candidate profile, match score
- Message candidates interested in your role
- Track candidate pipeline (viewed/contacted/interviewed/offered/hired)

**Key Features:**
- Candidate search with advanced filters
- Match score (how well candidate fits role)
- Profile preview (see full candidate information)
- Bulk actions (message multiple candidates at once)
- Candidate ratings from previous employers visible
- "Verified" badge shows legitimate candidate
- Save candidates for future openings

### 4. Messaging & Communication

**What:** Message candidates directly to discuss role, extend offers, schedule interviews

**Why It Matters:**
- Fast communication speeds hiring (reduce time-to-hire)
- All conversations tracked
- Secure platform (safe for both parties)
- Interview scheduling built-in

**How It Works:**
- Message matched candidates
- Receive notifications when candidates reply
- Schedule interviews (calendar integration)
- Send offer letter or pre-interview details
- See full conversation history
- Mark candidate status (interviewed, offered, hired, rejected)

**Key Features:**
- In-app direct messaging
- Email notifications of new messages
- Calendar integration (schedule interviews)
- Message templates ("request interview," "send offer")
- Offer letter template
- Automated reminders (follow up on pending interviews)

### 5. Verification

**What:** Verify your business is legitimate before posting jobs

**Why It Matters:**
- Job seekers feel safe applying (verified employer badge)
- Shows you're serious about hiring
- Reduces spam/fake employers on platform
- Increases application quality

**How It Works:**
- Submit business registration document
- Proof of business address
- Admin reviews (24-48 hours)
- Receive "Verified Employer" badge

**Key Features:**
- Document upload (secure)
- Multiple verification methods (business registration, tax ID, business license)
- Quick turnaround (24-48 hours)
- Badge display on company profile
- Re-verification annually

### 6. Ratings & Analytics

**What:** Get rated by candidates; see hiring analytics

**Why It Matters:**
- Ratings build your employer reputation
- Good ratings attract better candidates
- Analytics show which jobs/skills attract best candidates
- Data-driven hiring decisions

**How It Works:**
- Candidates rate you after job completion
- See aggregated ratings on your company profile
- View hiring analytics: applications by job, interviews conducted, time-to-hire, cost-per-hire
- Track hiring trends over time

**Key Features:**
- Company rating (aggregated from candidates)
- Hiring funnel analytics (views → applications → interviews → offers → hires)
- Candidate quality metrics
- Time-to-hire tracking
- Cost-per-hire calculation
- Performance reports by job type/location

## PLATFORM FEATURES (Both User Types)

### 1. Matching Algorithm

**What:** System recommends best job/candidate matches based on data

**Why It Matters:**
- Saves time scrolling through irrelevant options
- Improves quality of matches (right person for right job)
- Increases successful placements
- Better experience for both parties

**How It Works:**
- Algorithm compares candidate profile to job requirements
- Weights: skills match (40%), location match (30%), availability (15%), experience level (15%)
- Generates match score (0-100%)
- Recommends jobs to candidates, candidates to employers
- Learns from feedback (placements that worked, ratings given)

**Key Features:**
- Skills-based matching (does candidate have required skills?)
- Location-based matching (are they in preferred location?)
- Experience level matching (entry-level vs. experienced)
- Availability matching (start date, work type preference)
- Smart recommendations (daily job recommendations for job seekers)
- Passive notifications (alert when great match appears)

### 2. Trust & Safety System

**What:** Verification badges, ratings, and safety features build confidence

**Why It Matters:**
- Job seekers fear scams; verification proves legitimacy
- Employers fear unqualified candidates; verification proves credentials
- Ratings show who is trustworthy
- Community accountability

**How It Works:**
- Both parties must verify to use platform
- Verification badges visible on profiles
- Ratings from past interactions visible
- Report/block features for safety concerns
- Community moderation of reviews

**Key Features:**
- Verification badges (verified job seeker, verified employer)
- Ratings visible on profiles (aggregated, anonymous)
- Community trust score (based on interactions)
- Report/flag inappropriate behavior
- Block users (prevent future contact)
- Platform moderation (remove fake reviews, spam)
- Secure messaging (no personal contact details exposed)

### 3. Communication & Notifications

**What:** Stay updated on applications, messages, and job matches

**Why It Matters:**
- Both parties know where things stand
- Fast feedback reduces uncertainty
- Notifications keep people engaged
- Reduces ghosting (ignoring messages)

**How It Works:**
- Email notifications for important events
- In-app notifications for messages
- Job seeker: notification when application reviewed, interview scheduled, offer received
- Employer: notification when candidate applies, confirms interview, accepts offer
- Customizable notifications (frequency, types)

**Key Features:**
- Email notifications
- In-app notification center
- SMS notifications (optional)
- Notification preferences (turn on/off by type)
- Unsubscribe option
- "Do not disturb" mode

### 4. Resources & Support

**What:** Guides, templates, and resources to help both groups succeed

**Why It Matters:**
- Job seekers learn Canadian workplace norms
- Employers learn about hiring newcomers
- Reduces mismatches due to cultural gaps
- Increases success rates

**How It Works:**
- Job seekers: access guides on "working in Canada," interview tips, resume templates
- Employers: access guides on "hiring newcomers," onboarding best practices
- Templates: resume template, offer letter, interview questions
- FAQ: common questions answered
- Support: email support for issues

**Key Features:**
- Knowledge base (searchable articles)
- Resource library (downloads: resume template, interview guide, offer letter)
- Video tutorials (how to use platform)
- FAQ section
- Email support
- Community forum for peer advice
- Expert Q&A (HR professionals answering questions)

### 5. Analytics & Reporting

**What:** Track progress and measure success

**Why It Matters:**
- Job seekers see application status
- Employers measure hiring effectiveness
- Platform measures health (user satisfaction, placements, growth)

**How It Works:**
- Job seeker dashboard: applications sent, responses received, interviews scheduled, offers
- Employer dashboard: jobs posted, applications received, interviews conducted, hires made, cost-per-hire
- Platform metrics: total users, placements per month, average time-to-hire, user satisfaction

**Key Features:**
- Personal dashboard (applications, messages, saved jobs)
- Hiring analytics (funnel, time-to-hire, cost-per-hire)
- Success metrics (placements, retention, satisfaction)
- Reports (monthly, quarterly)
- Data exports (for record-keeping)

## Feature Philosophy

**MVP Priority:** Verify identity + enable matching + allow messaging = enable core job-to-hire transaction

**Growth Priority:** Build trust + enable community + provide resources = improve success rates

**Scale Priority:** Automate matching + enable partnerships + provide advanced tools = enable growth

Keep features focused on core mission: **Connect job-ready newcomers with employers who want to hire them.**
