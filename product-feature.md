# CanStart Product Roadmap

## Roadmap Overview

Three-phase roadmap from MVP to scale, with clear success metrics for each phase.

---

## Phase 1: MVP Launch (Months 1-3)

### Goal
Validate core product with early adopters; prove job seeker-to-hire conversion is possible.

### Features to Build

#### 1. Dual Registration System
- Job Seeker registration path
- Employer registration path
- Email verification
- Basic profile completion

#### 2. Job Seeker Profile
- Work history (past roles, companies, dates)
- Skills (searchable tags)
- Languages (fluency levels)
- Availability (start date, work type preferences)
- Profile photo
- Location preference

#### 3. Employer Profile
- Company information (name, size, industry)
- Location
- Company verification (business registration upload)
- Contact information
- Company description

#### 4. Job Posting (Employer)
- Job title
- Job description
- Requirements (skills, education, experience)
- Salary range
- Work type (entry-level, volunteer, part-time)
- Location
- Post/Edit/Delete job

#### 5. Job Search (Job Seeker)
- Browse all open jobs
- Filter by: location, job type, industry, skills
- Job details view
- "Apply" button
- Save/bookmark jobs

#### 6. Matching Algorithm
- Skills-based matching: Compare job requirements to seeker profile
- Location matching: Flag if within preference range
- Simple match score (0-100%) shown to both parties
- Recommend matched candidates to employers

#### 7. Messaging System
- In-app direct messaging between job seeker and employer
- Show unread message count
- Email notification of new messages
- Basic conversation history

#### 8. Verification System
- Job Seeker: ID verification + work permit upload (admin review)
- Employer: Business registration document (admin review)
- Verification status badge on profiles (verified / pending / unverified)
- Verification takes 24-48 hours

#### 9. Basic Admin Dashboard
- Approve/reject verifications
- Monitor job posts
- Basic analytics (registrations, messages, applications)

### What NOT to Build (Save for Phase 2)
- Advanced matching algorithms
- Ratings/review system
- Community forums
- Mobile app
- Payment processing
- Video interviews

### Success Metrics for Phase 1

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Job Seeker Registrations | 500 | Test market adoption |
| Employer Registrations | 50 | Test employer interest |
| Verification Rate | 70% | Prove verification viable |
| Job Postings | 100+ | Enough job supply |
| Applications | 300+ | ~3 per job |
| Successful Placements | 10+ | Prove model works |
| Messaging Volume | 2,000+ | Active engagement |
| Average User Rating | 4.0+ | Basic satisfaction |

### Timeline
- Week 1-2: Design and development planning
- Week 3-6: Core feature development (registration, profiles, posting)
- Week 7-8: Matching algorithm and messaging
- Week 9-10: Verification system and admin
- Week 11: Testing and bug fixes
- Week 12: Launch with closed beta (settlement agency partners)

---

## Phase 2: Trust & Community (Months 4-6)

### Goal
Build trust through verification and community features; increase engagement and retention.

### New Features

#### 1. Enhanced Verification
- Video ID verification option
- Background check integration (optional, for employers)
- Employment reference verification
- Language proficiency assessment
- Credential verification badges (for professional certificates)

#### 2. Ratings & Review System
- 5-star rating system (post-placement)
- Written review/feedback (optional)
- Rating prompt 2 weeks after hire date
- Ratings visible on profiles (anonymous, aggregated)
- "Verified Hire" badge once person has completed role

#### 3. Advanced Matching
- Location-based matching (radius from preference)
- Skills proficiency matching (not just skills present)
- Salary expectations matching
- Work schedule matching
- Personality/values matching (optional questionnaire)
- Smart recommendations (job seeker sees daily job matches)

#### 4. Resource Center
- "Working in Canada" guides (for job seekers)
- Interview preparation materials
- Resume template and tips
- Workplace culture guides (by industry)
- Language/communication tips
- FAQ and troubleshooting

#### 5. Job Seeker Communication Tools
- Email notifications for new matching jobs
- Application status updates
- Interview scheduling (calendar integration)
- Offer letter template

#### 6. Employer Reporting
- Hiring funnel analytics (views → applications → interviews → offers)
- Candidate quality metrics
- Time-to-hire tracking
- Cost-per-hire calculation

### Success Metrics for Phase 2

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Job Seeker Registrations | 3,000 | 6x growth from Phase 1 |
| Employer Registrations | 200 | 4x growth |
| Verification Rate | 80% | Improved completion |
| Monthly Placements | 50 | 5x from MVP |
| Average Rating | 4.5 | Improved satisfaction |
| User Retention (30-day) | 60% | Active users |
| Employer Paid Rate | 30% | 30% convert to paid tier |

### Timeline
- Month 1: Requirements and design
- Month 2: Build verification, ratings, advanced matching
- Month 3: Resource center, employer reporting
- Month 4: Testing, bug fixes, launch

---

## Phase 3: Scale & Support (Months 7-12)

### Goal
Enable rapid growth and improve user outcomes through community and partnerships.

### New Features

#### 1. Community Forum
- Discussion boards (by location, job type, country of origin)
- Success stories (case studies of placed users)
- Peer advice (job seekers helping job seekers)
- Moderation and safety rules
- Expert Q&A (settlement agents, HR professionals answering)

#### 2. Mobile App (iOS & Android)
- All core features from web
- Push notifications for job matches
- Mobile-optimized messaging
- Easy photo upload for verification

#### 3. AI-Powered Features
- Job recommendation engine (ML-based, not just skill matching)
- Resume scoring (feedback on resume quality)
- Interview coaching (AI feedback on responses)
- Salary prediction (for negotiations)

#### 4. Settlement Agency Partnerships
- White-label integration (agency clients use CanStart branded as agency tool)
- Referral tracking
- Partnership analytics
- API for data sharing (with consent)

#### 5. Employer Features
- Bulk job posting (CSV upload)
- Team hiring (multiple team members reviewing candidates)
- Hiring templates (job posting templates by role type)
- Batch messaging (message all qualified candidates at once)

#### 6. Expanded Resources
- Video interviews (candidates can pre-record interviews)
- Skills assessment tests (math, language, technical)
- Certification partners (integration with accredited skill courses)
- Mentor matching (connect newcomers with Canadian professionals for advice)

### Success Metrics for Phase 3

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Job Seeker Users | 15,000 | Reach critical mass |
| Employer Users | 1,000 | Healthy employer base |
| Monthly Placements | 200 | Significant impact |
| Platform Revenue | $300K+/month | Sustainable business |
| User Satisfaction (NPS) | 60+ | Strong loyalty |
| Retention (6-month) | 50% | Sticky platform |
| Partner Agencies | 20+ | Key distribution |

### Timeline
- Month 1-2: Community forum, settlement agency integration
- Month 3: Mobile app development
- Month 4: AI features (recommendations, coaching)
- Month 5: Additional employer tools
- Month 6: Polish, testing, national launch

## Success Indicators Across Phases

### Adoption Metrics
- **Phase 1:** Proof of concept (10 successful placements)
- **Phase 2:** Market traction (50+ monthly placements)
- **Phase 3:** Scale proof (200+ monthly placements)

### Quality Metrics
- **Phase 1:** 4.0+ average rating (basic satisfaction)
- **Phase 2:** 4.5+ rating (good satisfaction)
- **Phase 3:** 4.7+ rating (excellent satisfaction)

### Engagement Metrics
- **Phase 1:** 65% of users log in once
- **Phase 2:** 60% return within 30 days
- **Phase 3:** 50% return within 60 days, 30% monthly active

### Business Metrics
- **Phase 1:** $0 revenue (validation phase)
- **Phase 2:** $50K+/month (product-market fit)
- **Phase 3:** $300K+/month (profitable operations)

---

## Decision Gates Between Phases

Before moving to next phase, must validate:

**Phase 1 → Phase 2:**
- ✓ 10+ successful placements
- ✓ 4.0+ average rating
- ✓ 70%+ verification completion
- ✓ Employer willingness to pay for premium

**Phase 2 → Phase 3:**
- ✓ 50+ monthly placements
- ✓ 4.5+ average rating
- ✓ 60%+ 30-day retention
- ✓ 30%+ employer paying for premium
- ✓ Settlement agency partnerships expressing interest

---

## Key Principle

**Build iteratively, but with clear metrics.** Each phase builds on previous; don't move to next phase until previous shows clear success. This prevents wasting resources on features users don't want.

**Focus remains constant:** Connect job-ready newcomers with employers who want to hire them. Everything else supports that core mission.
