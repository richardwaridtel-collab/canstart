# CanStart – Job Portal for Canadian Newcomers

![Capstone](https://img.shields.io/badge/capstone-York%20University%202024-orange)

> **Connecting work-ready Canadian newcomers with employers who want to hire them.**

CanStart is a two-sided job marketplace solving the employment integration challenge facing 450,000+ annual Canadian newcomers. Despite valid work authorization, newcomers face a 6-month average job search—we've designed a platform that cuts that to weeks.

---

## 🎯 The Problem

- **450,000+ newcomers** arrive in Canada annually
- **Average job search: 6 months** (vs. 2-3 months for domestic candidates)
- **Catch-22:** Need "Canadian experience" to get a Canadian job, but can't get experience without a job
- **Trust gap:** Newcomers fear job scams; employers uncertain about foreign credentials
- **Market failure:** No platform optimized for this specific job-seeker × employer match

## ✅ The Solution

CanStart is a **verified, two-sided marketplace** that:

- ✓ Connects verified newcomers with employers willing to hire them
- ✓ Solves trust through mutual verification (work permit + business legitimacy)
- ✓ Speeds hiring from 3 months → 3 weeks through smart matching
- ✓ Reduces recruitment costs by eliminating 25-30% recruitment agency fees
- ✓ Builds community through ratings and resources

## 🏗️ Product Overview

### Core Features

**For Job Seekers:**
- Profile creation (work history, skills, languages)
- Smart job matching (skills + location + preferences)
- Direct employer messaging
- Identity + work permit verification
- Employer ratings and reviews

**For Employers:**
- Job posting and candidate search
- Verified candidate pool (pre-vetted credentials)
- Direct candidate messaging
- Hiring funnel analytics
- Reduced recruitment time and cost

**Platform:**
- Matching algorithm (skills, location, availability)
- Trust system (verification badges + community ratings)
- Secure messaging (no data exposure)
- Resources (Canadian workplace guides, interview tips)
- Analytics (placement tracking, success metrics)

### Product Roadmap

**Phase 1 (MVP):** Core platform - registration, profiles, matching, verification, messaging
**Phase 2 (Growth):** Trust & community - enhanced verification, ratings, advanced matching
**Phase 3 (Scale):** Community & partnerships - forums, mobile app, AI recommendations

[See full roadmap →](./docs/product/product-roadmap.md)
