# CanStart Product Overview

## One-Liner

A two-sided job platform connecting Canadian newcomers to small employers, solving the "6-month employment gap" that blocks career integration despite work authorization.

---

## The Problem (Why This Matters)

### The Context
- **450,000 newcomers** arrive in Canada annually
- **Average job search time: 6 months** (vs. 2-3 months for domestic candidates)
- Despite having **valid work permits** and **strong qualifications**, newcomers face systemic barriers

### Root Causes

**For Newcomers:**
1. **The "Canadian Experience" Paradox** – Employers demand Canadian work experience, but how do newcomers get it without a job?
2. **Limited Networks** – Professional networks built in home country don't transfer; starting from zero in Canada
3. **Resume Format Differences** – Canadian resume norms differ from other countries; hiring managers may not understand foreign formats
4. **Language Concerns** – Accent, proficiency, idiom understanding create perceived risk for employers
5. **Credential Recognition** – Foreign degrees/certifications may not be recognized without formal equivalency assessment
6. **Scam Fear** – Newcomers are targets for employment scams; legitimate opportunities are hard to distinguish from fraudulent ones

**For Small Employers:**
1. **Recruitment Costs** – Traditional recruitment agencies expensive for SMBs
2. **Hiring Speed** – Job posting → screening → interviews → hiring takes 2-3 months
3. **Credential Uncertainty** – How to assess if foreign credentials are legitimate?
4. **Cultural Fit Unknown** – Will they integrate into team? Understanding of Canadian workplace norms?
5. **Access Gap** – Limited pipeline to skilled entry-level candidates
6. **Hidden Diversity Wins** – Want to hire diverse talent but not connected to communities

### Market Opportunity

| Group | Pain Point | Market Size |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| Newcomers | 6-month job search despite work eligibility | 450,000/year × 80% actively seeking = 360,000 |
| Employers | Slow, expensive hiring process | ~100,000 SMBs in Canada hiring entry-level |
| **Total Addressable Market** | **Employment integration gap** | **~$1B+ annually in lost productivity** |

---

## The Solution

### What is CanStart?

**A two-sided marketplace that:**
1. **Connects** work-ready newcomers with legitimate employers
2. **Verifies** both parties (work permit + business legitimacy) to reduce scam risk
3. **Matches** candidates to opportunities using skills + location + preferences
4. **Enables** direct communication and quick hiring
5. **Builds** trust through ratings and community

### Why This Works

**For Job Seekers:**
- Access legitimate job opportunities without 6-month search
- Employers actively seeking newcomers (no "Canadian experience" barrier)
- Verified employers (safety from scams)
- Community support from other newcomers
- Fast feedback (know job status in days, not weeks)

**For Employers:**
- Access to skilled, motivated workforce
- Simplified hiring (post → match → interview → hire in 2-3 weeks)
- Pre-verified candidates (work permit confirmed)
- Reduced recruitment costs
- Meet diversity goals while solving hiring needs

### Core User Flows

**Job Seeker:**
Register → Create Profile → Search Jobs → Apply → Message Employer → Interview → Get Hired → Rate Experience

**Employer:**
Register → Verify Business → Post Job → Search Candidates → Message Candidates → Schedule Interviews → Hire → Rate Candidate

## Market Validation

### User Research Methodology
- **Total Participants:** 65+ (35+ job seekers, 30+ employers)
- **Interview Type:** Semi-structured, in-depth conversations
- **Duration:** 45-60 minutes per interview
- **Goal:** Understand pain points, validate platform concept, gauge interest

### Key Findings

**Job Seeker Segment (35+ interviews):**
- **Pain Point Confirmation:** 100% mentioned employment barriers (experience, networks, scams)
- **Platform Interest:** 75% said "This would help me get Canadian job faster"
- **Willingness:** 80% said "I would use this platform"
- **Urgency:** Average frustration level: 8/10 (high)

**Employer Segment (30+ interviews):**
- **Hiring Challenge:** 90% mentioned slow recruitment process
- **Hiring Cost:** Average recruitment cost per hire: $2,000-5,000
- **Hiring Timeline:** Average time-to-hire: 2-3 months
- **Platform Interest:** 80% said "This would help us find candidates"
- **Willingness:** 75% said "We would use this for hiring"

### Validation Signals
✓ Both user groups **spontaneously mentioned** core pain points (no leading questions)
✓ **75-80% interest rate** is strong market validation
✓ Employers willing to pay for faster hiring (freemium/premium model viable)
✓ Job seekers desperate for alternatives (engagement will be high)

## Business Model

### Revenue Options (To Be Validated)

**Option 1: Freemium (Recommended for MVP)**
- Job Seekers: Free access (drive volume)
- Employers: Free posting + Premium analytics/featured listings ($99-299/month)
- Target: 1,000 employers paying $150/month = $150K/month = $1.8M/year

**Option 2: Transaction-Based**
- Commission per successful hire: $500-1,000 per placement
- Target: 200 monthly placements × $750 = $150K/month = $1.8M/year

**Option 3: Hybrid (Payroll Integration)**
- Job Seekers: Free
- Employers: Free posting + commission on payroll processed through platform
- Target: $50-100 per paycheck processed

### Unit Economics (Freemium Model)
- Cost per user acquisition: $20 (job seeker), $100 (employer)
- Lifetime value: Employer avg 2 hires/year × $150/month × 12 = $1,800 LTV
- Payback period: 2 months (strong)
- CAC ratio: 1:9 (excellent)
