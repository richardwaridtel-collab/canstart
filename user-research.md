# CanStart – Job Portal for Canadian Newcomers

## Overview

CanStart is a two-sided job platform designed to connect work-ready newcomers to Canada with small business owners seeking entry-level and skilled talent. The platform directly addresses the employment integration challenge that 450,000+ newcomers face annually.

## The Problem

**The Employment Gap:**
- 450,000+ newcomers arrive in Canada annually
- Average job search time: **6 months** (vs. 2-3 months for domestic candidates)
- Despite work authorization, newcomers face significant barriers

**Key Barriers for Newcomers:**
- Lack of "Canadian work experience" (catch-22: need Canadian experience to get a job)
- Limited professional networks in Canada
- Unfamiliar resume/interview formats
- Language proficiency concerns
- Fear of job scams targeting vulnerable newcomers
- Credential recognition challenges

**Pain Points for Small Employers:**
- Traditional recruitment takes months (posting → screening → hiring)
- Difficulty assessing foreign credentials
- Uncertainty about cultural fit and work style
- Need for quick hiring turnaround
- Limited access to reliable entry-level talent

## The Solution

**CanStart creates a win-win marketplace:**

For Job Seekers:
- Access to legitimate, verified job opportunities
- Employers willing to hire newcomers without "Canadian experience" requirement
- Guidance on Canadian workplace culture
- Fast job matching (reduce 6-month search)
- Community of people with similar backgrounds

For Employers:
- Access to skilled, motivated workforce
- Simplified hiring process (no recruitment agency needed)
- Pre-verified candidates (identity + work permit confirmed)
- Reduced hiring time and cost
- Meet diversity and inclusion goals

## Key Metrics

**Market Validation:**
- User research conducted with **65+ participants** (35+ job seekers, 30+ employers)
- **75% of job seekers** said: "This platform would help me get Canadian experience faster"
- **80% of employers** said: "This would help me find qualified candidates with less effort"

**Market Size:**
- Total Addressable Market (TAM): 450,000 annual newcomers
- Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM): ~360,000 actively seeking employment
- Year 1 goal: 5,000 placements

## Core Product Features

### For Job Seekers
- **Profile Creation** – Build profile with work history, skills, language proficiency
- **Job Search** – Browse and filter opportunities by location, industry, job type
- **Application** – Apply directly through platform with one click
- **Messaging** – Direct communication with employers; schedule interviews
- **Verification** – Prove legal work permit status; build trust with employers
- **Ratings & Feedback** – Rate employers after placement; build community trust

### For Employers
- **Company Profile** – Create employer profile; build credibility
- **Job Posting** – Post volunteer or paid opportunities quickly
- **Candidate Search** – Browse qualified candidates; filter by skills/experience
- **Messaging** – Communicate with candidates; extend interview invites
- **Verification** – Verify as legitimate business; get "Verified Employer" badge
- **Ratings & Feedback** – Rate candidates; help other employers make decisions

### Platform Features
- **Matching Algorithm** – Skills-based, location-based, preference-based matching
- **Trust System** – Verification badges and public ratings build confidence
- **Secure Communication** – In-app messaging, video calls, document sharing
- **Analytics** – Employers see hiring funnel; job seekers track application status

## Product Roadmap

### Phase 1: MVP (Launch)
**Goal:** Validate core product with early adopters

Features: Registration, profiles, job posting/search, basic matching, verification, messaging
Success target: 500 job seekers, 50 employers, 100 job postings, 10 placements

### Phase 2: Trust & Community (Months 4-6)
**Goal:** Build trust through verification and community features

Features: Enhanced verification, ratings/reviews, advanced matching, resource center
Success target: 3,000 job seekers, 200 employers, 50 monthly placements

### Phase 3: Scale & Support (Months 7-12)
**Goal:** Enable growth and improve outcomes

Features: Community forum, mobile app, AI recommendations, settlement agency partnerships
Success target: 15,000 job seekers, 1,000 employers, 200 monthly placements
