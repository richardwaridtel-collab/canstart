# User Personas – CanStart

## Persona 1: Newcomer Job Seeker

### Profile
**Name:** Maya Patel
**Age:** 32
**Background:** Accountant from India with 8 years experience at Big 4 firm
**Canada Timeline:** Arrived 6 months ago on Open Work Permit
**Current Status:** Still job searching

### Goals
- Secure Canadian accounting job within next 2 months
- Build professional network in Canada
- Understand Canadian workplace expectations
- Earn stable income
- Eventually transition from permit to permanent residence

### Pain Points
1. **"Canadian Experience" Barrier** – Every job posting says "3+ years Canadian experience." How can I get it without a job?
2. **Limited Network** – In India, I had 100+ professional contacts. Here, I know 2 people in my field
3. **Credential Uncertainty** – Is my Indian accounting designation equivalent to Canadian CPA? What do employers think?
4. **Interview Anxiety** – Canadian interviews feel different. Canadians are more casual; I'm more formal. Am I doing it wrong?
5. **Resume Concerns** – My CV is 3 pages; I'm told Canadian résumés are 1 page. Will I be judged for this?
6. **Isolation** – I don't know anyone else going through this. The job search feels lonely

### Motivations
- Prove capability in Canadian context
- Contribute skills to Canadian economy
- Build new professional identity
- Support family financially
- Achieve permanent residency

### How CanStart Helps
- **Verified Employers** – Reduces scam fear; can trust platform
- **Newcomer-Friendly Employers** – Employers willing to hire without Canadian experience requirement
- **Faster Job Finding** – Reduce 6-month search to 2-3 months
- **Community Support** – Connect with others navigating same challenges
- **Clear Expectations** – Understand what employers actually need

### What Triggers Signup
- Sees CanStart ad on settlement agency website
- Hears about it from friend who successfully got job
- Desperate after 6 months of rejection

### What Keeps Her Engaged
- Sees relevant job match (accountant role, nearby)
- Employer responds quickly to application
- Gets interview within 1 week
- Community feature shows others with same background got hired

## Persona 2: Small Business Owner

### Profile
**Name:** David Chen
**Age:** 42
**Business:** Tech startup, 20 employees
**Location:** Toronto
**Business Timeline:** Company founded 4 years ago; growing rapidly
**Current Pain:** Need to hire 3 junior developers + 2 support staff ASAP

### Goals
- Hire qualified entry-level developers and support staff quickly
- Reduce hiring timeline from 3 months to 6 weeks
- Avoid $5,000+ per hire recruitment agency fees
- Build diverse team
- Retain good employees long-term

### Pain Points
1. **Slow Hiring** – Posting job on LinkedIn → screening → interviews → offer takes 3 months. Business can't wait that long
2. **Cost** – Recruitment agencies charge 25-30% of first year salary. For $60K hire = $15K commission. Untenable for 5 roles
3. **Candidate Quality** – LinkedIn flooded with overqualified/underqualified candidates; lots of noise
4. **Cultural Fit Unknown** – How do I know if person will fit our team before expensive interview process?
5. **Credential Uncertainty** – How do I verify if foreign degree/experience is legitimate?
6. **Onboarding Unknown** – If I hire someone new to Canada, will they need special support?

### Motivations
- Grow company through rapid hiring
- Control hiring costs
- Build strong, diverse team
- Improve hiring efficiency
- Reduce management burden of recruitment

### How CanStart Helps
- **Fast Matching** – Connect with pre-qualified candidates in days, not months
- **Pre-Vetted Pool** – Verified work permits and credentials = less risk
- **Lower Cost** – No 25-30% commission; direct hiring
- **Simplified Process** – Post job → match → interview → hire
- **Diverse Hiring** – Access to motivated newcomer pool; meets DEI goals
- **Willing Learners** – Newcomers motivated, eager to prove themselves

### What Triggers Signup
- Realizes hiring agency cost is unsustainable
- Sees business opportunity in specialized hiring platform
- Hears about CanStart from other business owner

### What Keeps Him Engaged
- Posts job and gets 10+ qualified matches within 2 days
- Can interview candidates within 1 week
- Successful hire completes 90-day probation successfully
- Can hire multiple positions quickly
- Saves $12K+ in agency fees per role

## Persona Tensions: How CanStart Navigates Competing Needs

### The "Experience vs. Motivation" Tension

**Maya's Need:** "I don't have Canadian experience, so don't judge me for it"
**David's Need:** "Hiring someone without Canadian experience is a risk; I need assurance"

**CanStart Solution:**
- Platform positions newcomers as "eager, motivated, underutilized talent"
- Employers get verified work permits + profiles showing drive/willingness
- Community ratings from other employers reassure about newcomers' capabilities
- Platform messaging: "Hire proven talent at the beginning of their Canadian journey"

### The "Speed vs. Thoroughness" Tension

**Maya's Need:** "I want fast feedback; waiting 3 weeks to hear back kills hope"
**David's Need:** "I need time to screen carefully; can't afford hiring mistake"

**CanStart Solution:**
- Smart matching reduces pool size (David only sees qualified candidates)
- Clear profiles give David confidence to interview faster
- Two-way communication speeds up process without rushing
- System nudges both parties to move forward (next steps reminders)

### The "Trust vs. Risk" Tension

**Maya's Need:** "I need to know employer is real and won't scam me"
**David's Need:** "I need to know candidate has legitimate credentials"

**CanStart Solution:**
- Mutual verification system (both need verified accounts)
- Ratings/reviews from both parties visible
- Platform holds communication channel (can't ask for upfront fees outside platform)
- Resources for both: "How to spot red flags" guides

## Key Insight: The Perfect Marketplace Match

**Maya's "Problem"** = (no Canadian experience) = **David's "Preference"** (cheaper, more trainable)

**Maya's "Desire"** = (legitimate, verified employer) = **David's "Need"** (vetted, reliable candidate)

This is perfect marketplace alignment. CanStart's job is connecting these two groups who need each other.
